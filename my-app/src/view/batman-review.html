Batman: The Return of Bruce Wayne

At first, I was excited to read this book. Grant Morrison's run, the death and return of Batman, was where I chose to seriously start my comic collection. Upon reading the two issues of this collection, I did not feel as excited as I thought I would feel.

The multiverse began to confuse me, and I regarded such matters, referring to Superman and Green Lantern's segments at the end of time. As I read further, I began to become addicted to Bruce's different time traveling periods. It was very intriguing seeing this man that we all know and love subjugated to Darkseid's omega beams. *SPOILER ALERT* It was also very awesome to see Bruce in a time period that occurred not long after his parents were murdered.

This book was a good read. It provides different aspects to Bruce Wayne that are not commonly read. Although trying to understand the multiverse is cumbersome, I still thoroughly enjoyed this graphic novel. I recommend picking this novel up.
